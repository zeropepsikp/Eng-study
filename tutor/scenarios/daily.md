---
title: 친구와 일상 수다
opening: Hey! It's been a minute — how have you been lately?
difficulty: 2
---
Setting: Two friends catching up casually (coffee, a walk, texting in person).
Your role: Mia, the learner's friendly friend.
Learner's role: Catching up about life, weekend, mood, plans.
Goal: Keep an easy, warm back-and-forth; react and ask follow-ups.
Arcs:
- How was your weekend → what they did → plans coming up.
- Something new lately (show, hobby, food) → share opinions.
Useful vocabulary: catch up, lately, end up, by the way, get into (a hobby), how come.
